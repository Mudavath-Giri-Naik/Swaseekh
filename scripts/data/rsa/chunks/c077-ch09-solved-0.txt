Ex. 4. Solve : 98.6 × 83.9 × 724.4 × 93.9 × 88.6 = 72?.	
(L.I.C., 2005)
	Sol.	 Let 98.6 × 83.9 × 724.4 × 93.9 × 88.6 = 72x.
	
	 Then, 9(8.6 + 3.9) × 8(3.9 + 8.6) × 724.4 = 72x
	
	 ⇔	
912.5 × 812.5 × 724.4 = 72x ⇔ (9 × 8)12.5 × 724.4 = 72x
	
	 ⇔	
7212.5 × 724.4 = 72x ⇔ 72(12.5 + 4.4) = 72x ⇔ 7216.9 = 72x ⇔ x = 16.9.

Ex. 5. Solve : (0.064) × (0.4)7 = (0.4)? × (0.0256)2.	
(Bank P.O., 2010)

	 ⇔

Ex. 18. If 8x . 2y = 512 and 33x + 2y = 96, then what is the value of x and y?	
(M.A.T., 2004)
Sol.	 8x 2y = 512 ⇔ (23)x . 2y = 29 ⇔ 23x + y = 29 ⇔ 3x + y = 9	
...(i)
	
And, 33x + 2y = 96 ⇔ 33x + 2y = (32)6 = 312 ⇔ 3x + 2y = 12	
...(ii)
	
Subtracting (i) from (ii), we get: y = 3.
	
Putting y = 3 in (i), we get: 3x = 6 or x = 2.
	
Hence, x = 2 and y = 3.
